About
I am a Software Engineer who is passionate about developing applications. From Simple websites, to more involved web apps, to Machine Learning projects, I enjoy it all. I first fell in love with the trade in middle school coding plugins for video games and developing my own personal website. I continued with side projects and took several classes throughout high school. Years later, I earned a B.S. in Computer Science from the Virginia Tech School of Engineering in May 2022. Now, I am employed as a Software Engineer for Lockheed Martin, and continue to work on several projects on the side.
I take great pride in my work and get a lot of statifaction from building useful things for people. While I enjoy the technical (coding) side of things I think my favorite part of the process is working with the customer and brainstorming solutions with teammates.
Besides coding I enjoy playing Guitar, Backpacking, and spending time with friends and family.
